Hanlin Ma is Associate Professor in the Department of Politics, School of Marxism, Suzhou University of Science and Technology, China.

His research interests include philosophy of science and technology, moral philosophy and ethics, philosophy of cognitive science, philosophy of technology, and the political philosophy of One Health. His recent work also addresses artificial intelligence, moral enhancement, free will, democratic education, and interdisciplinary teaching.

For citations and an updated publication record, see his [Google Scholar profile](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=VCPqE2MAAAAJ&hl=en).

Current research areas:

- Philosophy of science and technology
- Moral philosophy and ethics
- Philosophy of cognitive science
- Philosophy of technology
- Political philosophy of One Health

Education:

- B.Sc. in Applied Psychology, Jianghan University
- M.A. in Ethics, Huazhong University of Science and Technology
- Ph.D. in Philosophy of Science and Technology, Huazhong University of Science and Technology

Selected academic service:

- Associate Professor, Suzhou University of Science and Technology, 2017-present
- Council Member, Jiangsu Association for Dialectics of Nature
- Member, China General Artificial Intelligence Association
- Academic Committee Member, Higher Education Development Committee for Life-Care Education, ACTAsia

Courses taught include ethics of science and technology, dialectics of nature, philosophy of science, intelligent philosophy, and academic writing.
